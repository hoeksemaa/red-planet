# RP-8: Search bar: collapse/expand states + feature info card

Redesign search bar with two states: collapsed (just the input, minimal footprint) and expanded (full results list or feature info card). Clicking a map label populates the search bar and expands the feature card below it showing name, type, diameter, origin. Clicking away collapses to just the bar. Styled per RP-6 (Google Maps tokens). Depends on RP-4 (labels as a layer), RP-6 (design system).
